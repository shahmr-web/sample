Eid Salah Procedure

The prayer of al-Fitr is two rak'ahs. When praying at home it can be done in Jamaat or alone


  1. Raise the hands, say Allahuakbar and fold them
  2. Read sana.
  3. Raise the hands, say Allahuakbar and bring them hands down
  4. Raise the hands, say Allahuakbar and bring them hands down
  5. Raise the hands, say Allahuakbar and fold them
  6. Imam should read 'Aoothu','Bismillah','Fatiha','Surah'
